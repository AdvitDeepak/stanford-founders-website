Services

Programming, matching, mixers, and investor access

01

Founder programming

Stanford Founders runs a regular speaker series and workshops for members. Sessions cover fundraising, recruiting, go-to-market, and other operational topics, led by founders and partners.

  • Speaker series and firesides with operators
  • Fundraising, recruiting, and go-to-market workshops
  • Office hours with partners and angels
02

Co-founder matching

We host curated dinners and introductions across the graduate schools to help members find co-founders. Members are paired by background, sector, and stage.

  • Curated co-founder dinners
  • Profile-based introductions across schools
  • Office hours for matched pairs
03

Sector mixers

Sector-focused gatherings for members working in similar areas. Recent tracks include bio and therapeutics, climate and energy, defense, AI infrastructure, and fintech.

  • Bio & therapeutics
  • Climate & energy
  • Defense & deeptech
  • AI infrastructure
  • Fintech & payments
04

Investor access & Demo Day

Stanford Founders supports member fundraising through partner introductions and the annual Demo Day. Demo Day 2026 takes place on May 7 at the Tresidder Oak Lounge.

  • Demo Day each Spring, open to all members
  • Partner introductions at active venture firms
  • Pre-pitch reviews with alumni founders
